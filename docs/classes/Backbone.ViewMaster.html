Backbone.ViewMaster Class

Extends Backbone.View
Defined in: lib/backbone.viewmaster.js:24

Few tested opinions on how to handle deeply nested views in Backbone.js focusing on encapsulation and reusability.

https://github.com/epeli/backbone.viewmaster

NOTE: Most important methods setView, appendView, prependView and removeView are protected methods.
